Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique_Ingres

The potrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by French Neoclassical artist Jean-Auguste-Dominique_Ingres, and today hangs in Louvre. It is the third of the three potraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70cm
Home: Musée du Louvre, Paris
